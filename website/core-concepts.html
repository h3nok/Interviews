🎯 Core Concepts Overview

These four fundamental areas form the foundation of modern Generative AI. Master these concepts to build a solid understanding of the field.

🏗️
Transformer Architecture
The revolutionary architecture that changed AI forever. Understand self-attention, multi-head mechanisms, and positional encoding.
  • Self-Attention Mechanism
  • Multi-Head Attention
  • Positional Encoding
  • Encoder/Decoder Variants
  • Scaling Laws
 🤖
Large Language Models
Explore the evolution from GPT to modern LLMs. Learn about training, fine-tuning, and deployment strategies.
  • Model Evolution (GPT, BERT, T5)
  • Pre-training & Fine-tuning
  • Prompt Engineering
  • Evaluation Metrics
  • Deployment Challenges
 🎨
Diffusion Models
Understand the probabilistic framework behind image generation. From DDPM to modern diffusion techniques.
  • Forward & Reverse Processes
  • Noise Scheduling
  • DDPM & DDIM
  • Classifier-Free Guidance
  • Text-to-Image Generation
 🖼️
Multimodal AI
Bridge the gap between different data modalities. Learn about vision-language models and cross-modal understanding.
  • Vision-Language Models
  • Cross-Modal Attention
  • CLIP & DALL-E
  • Multimodal Fusion
  • Evaluation Strategies

🛤️ Recommended Learning Path

Step 1: Foundation

Start with Transformer Architecture to understand the core mechanisms that power modern AI.

Step 2: Language

Move to Large Language Models to see how transformers are applied to text generation.

Step 3: Generation

Explore Diffusion Models to understand probabilistic generation frameworks.

Step 4: Integration

Finally, study Multimodal AI to see how different modalities are combined.
